The Odds & Evens Game

Odd or Even is a game between two people whose goal is to randomly resolve a deadlock. Participants bet on even and odd. After that, both show their hands hiding some fingers. The fingers are counted, and the person who has correctly guessed if the total number of fingers is an even or odd number wins.

In this web app you can choose between 'evens' or 'odds'. There's no need to choose the number, as the app will assign automatically a random number between 1 and 5 for each oponent!
